Privacy Policy

Your Privacy Matters to Us

Introduction

Welcome to Krypts! We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our text encryption and decryption tool.

Information We Collect

Krypts is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect or store any of the following:

  • Encrypted or Decrypted Text: Your text is processed locally in your browser and never sent to our servers.
  • Passwords: Your encryption or decryption passwords are never stored or transmitted.
  • Personal Information: We do not require or collect any personally identifiable information (PII) to use our tool.

How We Use Your Information

Since we do not collect or store your data, there is no usage of your information beyond the local processing required to perform encryption and decryption.

Cookies and Tracking

Krypts does not use cookies or any tracking mechanisms. Your activity on our site is entirely private.

Third-Party Services

We do not share your data with third-party services. However, our tool relies on the Stanford JavaScript Crypto Library (SJCL) for encryption and decryption. You can review their GitHub repository for more information.

Data Security

Your data security is our top priority. Krypts uses 256-bit AES encryption, one of the most secure encryption standards available. All encryption and decryption processes occur locally in your browser, ensuring your data never leaves your device.

Your Rights

Since we do not collect or store your data, there is no need for data access, correction, or deletion requests. Your data remains entirely under your control.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and we encourage you to review it periodically.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
