How to decrypt text

  1. Enter the password which was used to encrypt your text
  2. Enter your encrypted text
  3. Click the Decrypt text button
Decrypt text

How your text is encrypted and decrypted

Text encryption and decryption uses the Stanford JavaScript Crypto Library with 256-bit AES encryption in CCM mode, encryption keys are derived from your password using PBKDF2 with a 1000 work factor.
