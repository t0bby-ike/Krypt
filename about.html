About Krypts

Your Trusted Tool for Secure Text Encryption and Decryption

What is Krypts?

Krypts is a free, online tool designed to help you securely encrypt and decrypt sensitive text using 256-bit AES encryption. Whether you're sharing confidential information, protecting personal data, or securing business communications, Krypts ensures your text remains private and accessible only to those with the correct password.

Why Use Krypts?

In today's digital age, data security is more important than ever. Krypts provides a simple yet powerful solution for:

  • Protecting Sensitive Information: Encrypt text to prevent unauthorized access.
  • Secure Sharing: Share encrypted text with confidence, knowing only the intended recipient can decrypt it.
  • No Data Storage: Krypts does not store your password or data, ensuring complete privacy.
  • Ease of Use: A user-friendly interface makes encryption and decryption quick and straightforward.

How Does Krypts Work?

Krypts uses the Stanford JavaScript Crypto Library (SJCL) to implement 256-bit AES encryption in CCM mode. Here's how it works:

  1. Encryption: Enter a password and the text you want to encrypt. Krypts generates a secure, encrypted version of your text.
  2. Decryption: Enter the same password and the encrypted text to retrieve the original content.
  3. Security: Encryption keys are derived from your password using PBKDF2 with a 1000 work factor, ensuring robust protection.

Key Features of Krypts

256-bit AES Encryption

Krypts uses industry-standard 256-bit AES encryption, one of the most secure encryption methods available.

Password Protection

Your encrypted text is protected by a password of your choice, ensuring only authorized users can decrypt it.

Cross-Platform Compatibility

Access Krypts from any device with a web browser, making it a versatile tool for all your encryption needs.
